Our Standard

The MMAP Methodology —
Sponsorship Accountability That's Quantifiable

Every engagement Listen Media delivers is governed by the Marketing Accountability Standards Board's Marketing Metric Audit Protocol — the global gold standard for independently validated, transparently reported, and commercially defensible sponsorship investment.

Stage 1 — Media Equivalency & Beyond

We begin where most agencies stop — measuring logo impressions, broadcast mentions, and AVE — then move immediately to independently verified third-party audience data. We do not rely on property-provided estimates alone.

Stage 2 — Mid-Funnel Return on Objectives

Aligned with MASB's Sponsorship Accountability Metrics project, we track mid-funnel outcomes: brand awareness lift, sponsor recall, audience quality scores, and brand affinity shifts — quantified, not asserted.

Stage 3 — Financial Attribution & Business Impact

For advanced mandates, we build financial attribution systems showing how sponsorship investment links to commercial outcomes — sales lift, market share movements, and brand equity appreciation aligned to ISO 20671 standards.

Brand Finance Valuation — Our Next Phase

We are building commercial valuation capability on the Brand Finance global framework — enabling ISO-standard brand and property valuations that speak the language of CFOs, investors, and boards. Africa's first sports-specialist brand valuation firm.

MASB Current Projects — Active Alignment

Our services actively integrate MASB's live research: Brand Evaluation Standards (BES), Financial Value of Brands (FVB), Sponsorship Accountability Metrics (SAM), and the Universal Marketing Dictionary — every metric we use is internationally standardised.

The MMAP Audit Protocol

The Marketing Metric Audit Protocol defines how marketing metrics should be identified, validated, and applied. Listen Media is East Africa's first sports agency to embed MMAP into its standard service delivery — giving brands and investors the accountability they demand.
